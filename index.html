About Me

Hi, I am Yogesh Kumar, a B.Tech graduate in Internet of Things (IoT) with hands-on experience in Embedded Systems, Full-Stack IoT development, and Web Technologies. I specialize in designing end-to-end IoT solutions, starting from sensor interfacing and firmware development to cloud integration and user-facing dashboards. I have worked extensively with ESP32, STM32, Raspberry Pi, and communication protocols such as MQTT, HTTP, UART, SPI, I2C, and CAN. Alongside embedded development, I build responsive web applications and dashboards using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, Firebase, and SQL, enabling real-time monitoring and control of IoT systems. I have experience developing Qt/QML-based graphical dashboards, OTA update systems, and cloud-connected applications. I am passionate about creating practical, scalable, and user-focused technology solutions, especially in the domains of Electric Vehicles, IoT automation, and real-time telemetry systems.
